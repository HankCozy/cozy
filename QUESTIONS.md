# Let's Get Cozy Profile Questions

## Intro & Identity

1. Can you tell me your name—and is there a good story behind it?
2. You've heard the expression that age is just a number. How old are you in your head? What makes you say that?
3. Where were you born and raised? Where else have you lived along the way?
4. Do you have a current co-pilot on life's journey, and who makes up your close family?

## Free Time & Interests

5. When you're not busy, how do you enjoy spending your free time? Got any hobbies or passions?
6. What are you watching, reading, or listening to these days?
7. What's a favorite place you've traveled—or a dream destination you'd love to visit—and why?
8. Are you part of any groups, clubs, or organizations that you're passionate about?
9. Tell me about something that brings you joy:

## Relationships & Reflections

10. Who is your best friend, and what three words would they use to describe you?
11. Would you call yourself more of a homebody or a social butterfly—and why?
12. Who was one of the greatest influences on your life, and how did they shape you?
13. Tell me something about you that most people don't know – a hidden skill, an interesting life experience, an accomplishment.

## You & Your Community

14. What's one thing you want your community members to know about you?
15. Finally, what does being a part of this community mean to you?
